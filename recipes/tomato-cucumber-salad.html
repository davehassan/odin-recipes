Tomato Cucumber Salad

This is a very simple salad. Because I am a culinary child, it is one of the only ways in which I will eat vegetables

Ingredients

  • Tomato
  • Cucumber
  • White vinegar
  • Oregano
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Chop vegetables
  2. Toss with vinegar and spices
  3. Salt and pepper to taste
